FingerPrint as a Service (FAAS)

FAAS is a Javascript SDK that queries browser attributes and computes a hashed visitor identifier from them. FAAS can collect information in both normal or incognito mode even when browser purged. FAAS cover desktop, mobile, iPad, tablet and most of browser like safari, firefox, chrome.

Base on fingerprintID that FAAS generated, the client who integrates with FAAS will know exactly which user is visiting their website. Users just access the website and FAAS can detect who they are base on the information about their device, browser. FAAS already available on production with a dashboard page:

https://fingerprinting.geocomply.com/
